Social Mobile App

HotSpot Mobile

A nightlife discovery app helping users find events, track crowds, and explore venues in real-time.

Role

Lead Designer

Timeline

2–3 Months

Tools

Figma

Project Type

Mobile App

Problem Statement

Users struggled to gauge the true vibe and crowd level of nightlife venues before physically arriving. There was no reliable way to see "what's happening now" across multiple venues in a city.

The Challenge

Visualizing real-time "heat" and energy levels of venues without relying on outdated photos or reviews.

Final Design

The final UI leverages a vibrant, dark-themed aesthetic with energetic neon accents to match the nightlife context.

Expected Impact

  • Created an engaging, vibrant map-centric interface that provides real-time heatmaps and social updates.
  • Reduced "venue hopping" by providing accurate crowd density information.
  • Enhanced user safety through verified live check-ins and venue alerts.
