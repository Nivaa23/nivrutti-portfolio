Web Design Conference

PUNCCRT

An informative and sleek conference website designed for a seamless user experience, registration, and session scheduling.

Role

Lead Designer

Timeline

2–3 Weeks

Tools

Figma

Project Type

Web Design

Problem Statement

Scientific conferences often have information-heavy websites that are difficult to navigate, making it hard for attendees to find key deadlines and registration details. The visual design often feels dated and unprofessional.

The Challenge

Balancing high-density information with a modern, high-end aesthetic that appeals to an international academic audience.

Final Design

The final UI prioritizes legibility and a logical flow of information, using a dark, professional color palette with precision-aligned elements.

Expected Impact

  • Delivered a modern, clean architecture that prioritized essential information.
  • Led to a much smoother registration flow for international attendees.
  • Increased transparency regarding session times and venue details.
