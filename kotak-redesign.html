Fintech Redesign

Kotak Mahindra Bank App

A simplified and user-friendly redesign of a banking app focusing on clarity, accessibility, and seamless transactions.

Role

Lead Designer

Timeline

2–3 Months

Tools

Figma

Project Type

Mobile App Redesign

Problem Statement

The existing banking app was feature-heavy, visually cluttered, and made core actions like transferring money tedious. Users often felt overwhelmed by the number of menu options and technical jargon.

The Challenge

Reducing legacy complexity while maintaining all necessary banking functionalities for power users.

Final Design

The redesigned UI focuses on whitespace, intuitive iconography, and a clear primary action path for frequent tasks.

Expected Impact

  • Redefined the banking experience into an intuitive, accessible layout.
  • Minimizing the steps required for essential banking operations by 40%.
  • Improved accessibility through better color contrast and larger touch targets.
