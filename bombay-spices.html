Dashboard Web App

Bombay Spices

A retail grocery business looking to improve customer engagement and loyalty through a digital platform.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

3-4 Weeks

Tools

Figma

Project Type

Internship Project

Problem Statement

Retail businesses often struggle to maintain long-term customer engagement and encourage repeat purchases. Without a structured loyalty program, customers have limited incentives to return regularly.

Key Issues Identified:

  • Customers had no visibility into loyalty rewards
  • Promotions were not personalized
  • Businesses lacked insights into customer purchase behavior
  • Engagement between customers and the brand was limited

Why This Matters

Customer loyalty programs help businesses retain customers, increase repeat purchases and build long-term relationships.

Design Goals

  • Create a digital loyalty program
  • Improve customer engagement with the brand
  • Provide visibility into rewards and offers
  • Enable personalized promotions

Ideation & Exploration

The ideation phase focused on designing a platform that makes loyalty rewards easy to understand and redeem.

  • Digital reward tracking
  • Personalized tracking
  • Simple loyalty point redemption system

Wireframes

Wireframe layouts exploring prominent reward visibility and structured promotional offers.

Final Design

The final design focuses on improving customer engagement through a digital loyalty experience.

Expected Impact

  • Increased customer retention
  • Higher engagement with promotions
  • Better visibility into customer behavior

Reflections & Learnings

This project helped me understand how loyalty systems can influence customer behavior and drive repeat purchases.
