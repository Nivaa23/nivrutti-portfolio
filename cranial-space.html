Cranial Space

A platform designed to help UI/UX designers grow through structured feedback, community-driven critique and real-world design challenges.

Role

Product Designer / Founder

Timeline

Ongoing

Tools

Figma, Notion

Project Type

Startup Product

Problem Statement

Many aspiring designers face a major challenge after learning design fundamentals — they don't know how to improve further. While there are many platforms for sharing work, very few provide structured feedback or mentorship that helps designers understand what they should improve.

Key Issues Identified:

  • Designers struggle to receive high-quality, actionable feedback
  • Most platforms focus on showcasing work rather than improving it
  • There is no clear growth framework for designers
  • Beginners often feel lost after completing online courses
  • Portfolio development lacks structured critique and iteration

Why This Matters

Design growth happens through feedback and iteration, not just creating projects. Without proper critique, designers often repeat the same mistakes and struggle to progress to a professional level.

Product Vision

The vision behind Cranial Space is to create a growth ecosystem for designers where learning, feedback and portfolio development work together.

Mission: To empower UI/UX designers through community-driven critique, structured growth opportunities and industry-relevant exposure.

Vision: To become the global launchpad for aspiring UI/UX designers by creating a nurturing environment that bridges learning, feedback and career acceleration.

Elevator Pitch: Cranial Space is a design-focused platform that helps UI/UX designers grow faster through structured feedback, community engagement and portfolio-building tools.

Research & Insights

To understand the challenges designers face, I analyzed common patterns across design communities, portfolio platforms and learning platforms. Key insights included:

  • Designers often rely on random feedback from social media
  • Most critiques are subjective and inconsistent
  • Portfolio platforms focus on presentation rather than improvement
  • Many designers want structured learning after courses

Opportunity

There is an opportunity to create a feedback-driven design growth platform rather than just another portfolio-sharing platform.

Ideation & Product Strategy

The goal during ideation was to design a platform that actively helps designers improve, rather than just showcase their work. The platform structure was designed around three main pillars: Community Critique, Structured Growth, and Portfolio Development.

  • A UX Scoring system to evaluate design work
  • Structured critique frameworks for feedback
  • Design challenges based on real-world briefs
  • Exportable critique reports that designers can attach to portfolios
  • Mentorship-driven growth paths

Key Features

Community UX Scoring System: Designers receive structured feedback and scoring.

Community Critique Loop: A feedback system where designers review each other’s work.

Real-World Design Challenges: Industry-style design briefs to build strong portfolios.

Portfolio-Ready Critique Reports: Exportable design feedback

Expected Impact

  • Faster skill improvement for designers

Reflections & Learnings

Cranial Space represents my exploration into product design beyond individual interfaces. Through this project, I learned how to think about product strategy, community-driven platforms, designing systems rather than just screens, and creating structured growth experiences for users. Building Cranial Space helped me understand how design can shape learning ecosystems and communities.
