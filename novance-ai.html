Fintech Mobile App

Novance AI

An AI-powered financial assistant designed to help users better understand their credit health and financial behavior.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

4 Weeks

Tools

Figma

Project Type

Internship Project

Problem Statement

Many individuals struggle to understand their financial health due to complexity of financial reports, credit scores and transaction data. Most financial platforms provide raw financial information but do not clearly explain how users can improve their financial standing or manage their spending behavior effectively.

Key Issues Identified:

  • Financial data is difficult for users to interpret
  • Credit reports provide limited actionable insights
  • Users lack visibility into spending patterns
  • Financial recommendations are often unclear or generic

Why This Matters

Understanding financial health is critical for making informed decisions about credit usage, loans and financial planning. If users cannot easily interpret their financial data, they may struggle to improve their credit score.

Design Goals

  • Simplify complex financial data
  • Provide clear insights into credit health
  • Enable users to track spending patterns
  • Deliver actionable financial recommendations
  • Build trust through transparent and secure data handling

Final Design

The final design focuses on presenting financial data in a structured and intuitive interface.

Expected Impact

  • Better understanding of credit health
  • Improved financial decision-making
  • Greater transparency

Reflections & Learnings

This project helped me explore the challenges of designing financial products that deal with sensitive and complex data. Through this process, I learned how important clarity, trust and data visualization are when designing fintech applications.
